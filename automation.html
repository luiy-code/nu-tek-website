The Problem

Sound familiar?

Every week, your team re-types the same data into two systems, chases the same approvals by email, sends the same follow-up messages, and builds the same invoices by hand. None of it grows the business — it's just friction. And every manual step is a chance for a typo, a missed invoice, or a forgotten customer.

Our Approach

How we fix it

We sit with your team, map how work actually flows through the business, and identify the steps a computer should be doing. Then we build automations using tools you likely already pay for — Power Automate in Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, Zapier — so there's no new expensive software. Every automation comes documented, with error alerts, so you're never wondering whether it ran.

What's Included

What you get

  • Workflow mapping session — we chart how work moves through your business today
  • Automation opportunity report ranked by hours saved
  • Build-out of your highest-value flows (invoicing, follow-ups, approvals, data sync)
  • Error alerts so you know immediately if something needs attention
  • Plain-English documentation of every flow
  • Team walkthrough so your staff trusts and understands the automation

The Problem

Sound familiar?

You need real technical skills — someone to run the help desk, build a tool, clean up the network, wrangle your data. But a full-time hire is a salary, benefits, and a recruiting process you don't have time for. And if the workload is a project or a busy season, a permanent hire is the wrong shape entirely.

Our Approach

How we fix it

We keep a bench of vetted IT professionals — help desk, sysadmin, developers, data specialists — and match them to the role you actually need, for the duration you actually need. We handle the scoping, the matching, and the check-ins; you get a capable person embedded in your team without recruiting fees or long-term lock-in. Scale up when work spikes, scale down when it doesn't.

What's Included

What you get

  • Needs assessment — we define the role, skills, and hours honestly
  • Matching from our vetted bench (help desk, sysadmin, developer, data roles)
  • Onboarding support so the specialist is productive in days, not months
  • Regular check-ins with you on quality and fit
  • Flexible scaling — add hours, add people, or wind down anytime
  • No recruiting fees and no long-term contracts

The Problem

Sound familiar?

Customers message at 9pm and get silence until morning — sometimes they've bought elsewhere by then. Your team answers the same twenty questions every week. Quotes, drafts, and research eat hours. Big companies solved this with AI; most small businesses assume it's out of reach or too risky.

Our Approach

How we fix it

We find the two or three places where AI genuinely pays off for your business — usually customer questions, lead qualification, and internal drafting — and build scoped agents for exactly those jobs. Agents are trained on your documents, prices, and policies, so they answer like your business, not like a generic chatbot. Every agent has guardrails: it knows what it's allowed to say, and it hands off to a human the moment a conversation needs one.

What's Included

What you get

  • AI opportunity assessment — where AI will actually pay off in your business
  • Agent build trained on your documents, services, and pricing
  • Guardrails and human handoff — the agent escalates when it should
  • Integration with your website, WhatsApp, or email
  • A pilot period with weekly tuning based on real conversations
  • Plain-English report on what the agent handled and what it escalated
